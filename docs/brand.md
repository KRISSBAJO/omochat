# Omochat Brand

Omochat should feel calm, capable, and a little sweet.

## Palette

- Charcoal: `#171613`
- Graphite: `#3b3832`
- Sweet cream: `#f6edce`
- Honey: `#f5d84f`
- Lilac: `#d9cef4`
- Blush: `#f1d6e7`
- Cloud: `#fbfaf4`
- Glass: `#f7f4ec`

Use the lighter tones for atmosphere and surfaces, honey for small actions and highlights, and charcoal for primary actions and readable contrast.

## Product Feel

The app should stay clean and fast. Avoid noisy decoration. Use soft color in focused areas like avatars, status accents, empty states, and subtle backgrounds.
